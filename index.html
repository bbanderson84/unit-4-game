Crystal Collector Game!


At the start of the game, you will be given a random number.

Click on the four crystals below to generate a specific amount of points to your score, each crystal is worth a different amount of points.

To win the game, you must match your total points exactly with the given random number, and if your points go over the random number, you lose the game.

Each crystal's value will be given once you click on it. The values of each reset with every new game.
